Ammo Type

Bullets have a a damage value when hitting body parts, as well as a penetration value when hitting armor.

Here is a more detailed explanation all the calibers and there damage/penetration values in Escape from Tarkov. Ballistics

The 9x19 mm caliber has the PsT gzh bullet. This bullet has high damage, but poor armor penetration.

The PBP gzh bullet, however, has higher armor penetration and is better at damaging high armored targets.

Name Damage Penetration Armor Damage
PsT gzh 54 20 33
PBP gzh 52 39 53

The armor someone is wearing determines if the bullet will penetrate the armor or not.

Armor ranges from Class 1 Armor and up to Class 6 Armor.

PsT gzh round has a hard time penetrating Class 4 and above, whereas PBP can damage the armor and penetrate it.

Depending the helmet and armor someone is wearing, you'd have to assess what bullet your using and where to shoot them.

Arms and legs are the most exposed body part when someone has armor on.

If you are using high damage, low armor penetration you would aim for the legs
